Features

Continuously Capture AWS Services Usage Data
Aphelion collects AWS services usage limits across multiple AWS accounts. By using Trusted Advisor (TA) and direct AWS APIs, it collects the limits for various different AWS services/resources. The data is captured daily to make sure you stay up to date.
Visualize in Graphs and Charts
Aphelion visualizes the data collected by the backend service using a graph and table. You can quickly determine which AWS services/resources are at risk of reaching the service limit across multiple accounts within your environment.
Stay Alerted on Limit Thresholds to Eliminate Risk
Aphelion highlights the AWS services/resources that are at risk and gives warning based on percentage. This help enterprises to stay alert and eliminate the risk of impacting their critical business operations.
